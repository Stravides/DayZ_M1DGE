🍩🧟 DONUTS ZOMBIES 🍩

They had names.
They had lives.
Now… they are the infection.

A deadly disaster has left 88 infected wandering across Chernarus — 62 males and 26 females.
From John to Maria… from Michael to Emma…

🍩 WHAT MAKES THIS MOD DIFFERENT

This is not just another zombie pack.

Every infected carries visual storytelling:

🔪 Weapons embedded in their bodies
🩸 Signs of past battles
👤 Unique identity through names and variations

This mod transforms DayZ into a more alive, brutal, and believable experience.

⚠️ IMPORTANT
Attached weapons are visual only
